My Favourite Quotes

"Suffering is the difference between expectation and reality."

“You're going to be happy”, said life, “but first I'll make you stronger”

- Paulo Coelho

विद्या ददाति विनयं विनयाद् याति पात्रताम् ।
पात्रत्वात् धनम् आप्नोति धनाद् धर्मं ततः सुखम् ॥

- Hitopadesha

Rough translation: Knowledge makes one humble, humility begets worthiness, worthiness creates wealth and enrichment, enrichment leads to right conduct, and right conduct brings contentment.

कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते मा फलेषु कदाचन।
मा कर्मफलहेतुर्भूर्मा ते सङ्गोऽस्त्वकर्मणि॥

- Bhagavad Gita, Chapter 2, Verse 47

Translation: You have the right to work only but never to its fruits. Let not the fruits of action be your motive, nor let your attachment be to inaction.
